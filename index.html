FAQ

Everything you need to know

What is coworking?
Coworking is a style of work that involves a shared workplace, often an office, and independent activity. It's about community and collaboration.
What services are offered?
We offer shared workspaces, private offices, virtual offices, high-speed internet, and unlimited specialty coffee.
How to book space?
You can book through our website, mobile app, or by visiting our Kochi and Kozhikode locations directly.
What type of spaces are we talking about?
We have flexible desks, private studio suites, soundproof pods, and high-tech boardrooms.
Can I host an event at HeadQ?
Yes, we have dedicated event halls perfect for product launches, workshops, and networking mixers.
